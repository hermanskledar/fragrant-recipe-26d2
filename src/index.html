About Spartacus

Founded in 2012, T.S.K.V. Spartacus has grown to become the largest student strength sports association in the Benelux. We are dedicated to promoting strength sports among students at Tilburg University.

Our association is entirely student-led, creating a vibrant community where both beginners and experienced athletes can thrive. All our activities are conducted in English, making us accessible to international students.
